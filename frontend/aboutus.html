About Smart Study AI

Smart Study AI is a study support platform designed to help students organise their learning resources, manage notes, practise questions, and track their study progress.

Our Purpose

The purpose of Smart Study AI is to give students a clear and simple place to manage their study work. Instead of keeping notes, files, and practice tasks across different apps or folders, students can use one workspace to keep everything more organised.

Why This Project Matters

Many students find it difficult to stay organised while studying for different subjects. Smart Study AI aims to reduce that problem by providing a structured dashboard where students can access study materials, saved notes, practice questions, and progress tools.

Current Sprint Focus

In Sprint 1, the focus is on building the foundation of the platform. This includes account registration, the public landing page, and the starting layout for future student dashboard features.

Future Improvements

Future versions can include AI-based study recommendations, uploaded material analysis, personalised practice questions, reminders, and progress-based study suggestions.
