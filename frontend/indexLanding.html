Smart Study AI is a study support platform designed to help students organise their notes, materials, practice questions, and study progress in one place.

It is designed for students who want a clearer and more structured way to manage their learning resources and study tasks.

Sprint 1 focuses on the foundation of the platform, including account registration, public landing pages, and early user interface features.

What you can do

Study Materials

Keep uploaded learning resources organised in one place.

Saved Notes

Store important notes and study summaries for quick revision.

Practice Questions

Access basic practice questions to support active recall and efficiency.

Progress Tracking

View study progress and track your future goals.
